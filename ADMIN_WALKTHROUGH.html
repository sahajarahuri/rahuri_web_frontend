Admin walkthrough

A single-page guide for updating the Rahuri Sahaja Yoga website — no code needed.
॥ जय श्री माताजी ॥

Signing in

  1. Open the website in a browser and add /edit at the end of the address — e.g. rahurisahajyoga.com/edit.
  2. Sign in with the admin email and password.
  3. You will see six tabs at the top: Announcements · Sessions · Page Content · Resources · Testimonials · Site Settings.
Bookmark /edit in your browser so it opens with one tap.

Posting a new announcement (Puja, program, news)

  1. Click the Announcements tab, then New announcement.
  2. Type the title in English, and again in Marathi.
  3. Add a description in both languages.
  4. Optionally pick an event date from the calendar.
  5. Add media (see the box on the right).
  6. Click Publish.
Turn on Pin to top for the most important announcement — it shows first.

Adding media

  • Add Photo → click Upload photo, pick from your phone/laptop. The site compresses it automatically.
  • Add YouTube → paste a link like youtu.be/… or youtube.com/watch?v=…. The player appears automatically.
  • Add Link → give it a button label (e.g. Register here) and paste the URL.
  • Add Video → paste a direct video URL (use YouTube instead whenever possible).

Every Thursday, before the online meditation

  1. Open the Site Settings tab.
  2. Paste this week's live-stream link into Weekly Thursday YouTube URL.
  3. Click Save settings.
This single change updates two buttons on the homepage — "Join this Thursday" (inside the announcement) and "Join virtual session" (near the schedule). You do not need to touch anything else.

Editing testimonials ("Seekers speak")

  1. Open the Testimonials tab.
  2. Click Add, type the quote, author name, and location.
  3. Save. 2–4 testimonials look best on the homepage.

Updating social links or self-realisation video

  1. Open Site Settings.
  2. Update the YouTube channel, Facebook, or Instagram URL. Blank fields simply hide from the footer.
  3. The self-realisation video is what the hero button opens — a Shri Mataji video is set by default. Change only if the committee prefers a different one.

Common things to remember

  • Pin no more than one or two announcements at a time.
  • Photos from a phone are fine — the site handles resizing automatically.
  • For videos, always prefer YouTube — it's free and better quality.
  • To hide something without deleting, turn its Active toggle off.
